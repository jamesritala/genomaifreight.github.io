Road Freight Services

Full Truckload (FTL) - Dedicated truck for your exclusive use, ideal for large or bulk shipments. Container Haulage and delivery: Transportation of containers across South Africa and cross border into Africa ​ Less Than Truckload (LTL) - Cost-effective option for smaller shipments, consolidated with other cargo on the same truck. Abnormal Load Transport - Movement of oversized cargo within South Africa or cross border into Africa Oversized Load Permits - Securing permits and handling logistics for oversized cargo transport. Door-to-Door Delivery - Convenient pick-up and delivery at origin and destination locations.

Sea Freight Services

Full Container Load (FCL) - Cost-effective for large shipments, where you have exclusive use of a container. Less Than Container Load (LCL) - Ideal for smaller shipments, consolidated with other cargo for cost savings. Break Bulk Cargo - Handling oversized or oddly shaped cargo that doesn't fit in a standard container. Roll-on/Roll-off (RoRo) - Efficient for wheeled cargo like vehicles and trailers. Customs Clearance - Expert navigation of import and export regulations to expedite your shipment. Cargo Insurance - Protection against loss or damage during sea transport. Inland Transportation - Connecting your cargo from origin to final destination, including pick-up and delivery. Documentation Management - Handling all necessary paperwork for seamless customs clearance.

Air Freight Services

Express Air Freight - Fast and reliable delivery for urgent shipments, ideal for perishables or high-value goods. Scheduled Air Freight - Cost-effective option for time-sensitive cargo on established routes. Charter Services - Customized air charters for oversized or high-security cargo. Customs Clearance - Streamlined customs processing to minimize delays at airports. Cargo Insurance - Tailored insurance options to protect against risks during air transport. Door-to-Door Delivery - Seamless service from origin pick-up to final destination delivery. ​
